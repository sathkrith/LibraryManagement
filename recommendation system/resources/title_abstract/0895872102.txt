title:The Hauntings of Williamsburg, Yorktown, and Jamestown
category:History
abstract:For more than 250 years, people have told tales of strange occurrences in Virginia's historic triangle. Colonial homes, deserted battlefield, haunted cemeteries, local churches, and public buildings all provide backgrounds for ghostly stories. Jackie Behrend, creator of two of the area's most popular walking tours--"Haunted Williamsburg" and "Hauntings of Yorktown"--now gathers over 40 of her most popular tales in this collection.

title:Prescription for Terror
category:Fiction
abstract:When rape and murder strike young, single women on the sun-drenched San Diego coast, police are stumped. One of the victims was Dr. Cory Cohen's patient. The trauma triggers flashbacks of Cory's own terrifying rape. Her attacker went free, but she vows this one will not. Armed with psychological expertise, she is determined to uncover his identity. In her relentless pursuit, she risks the loss of an important friendship, and worse, places herself squarely into the killer's path.

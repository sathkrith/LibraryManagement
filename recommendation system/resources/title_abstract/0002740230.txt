title:Keep It Simple
category:Self-Help
abstract:Here is a guide to make life simpler, easier and more enjoyable. Two basic rules are offered: quietness and simplicity. They invite us to reject the quick fix, to stop talking so much, advance by retreat and live more profoundly. Practical ideas include the use of the TV, purging your wardrobe and booting out clutter. It contains tips and hints for a more manageable life.

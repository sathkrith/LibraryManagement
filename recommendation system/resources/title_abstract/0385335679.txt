title:Kissing in Manhattan
category:Fiction
abstract:At the Preemption, a mysterious apartment building with a prophetic doorman named Sender, the destinies of an offbeat group of tenants intertwine with the lives of three characters--James Branch, a shy accountant attached to the building's antique elevator; Wall Street lothario Patrick Rigg; and Rally McWilliams, an aspiring writer searching for a soulmate, in a collection of interlinked stories. Reissue. 45,000 first printing.

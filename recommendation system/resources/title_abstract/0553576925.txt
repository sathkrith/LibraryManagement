title:Hiding in the Shadows
category:Fiction
abstract:Awaking after a lengthy coma, accident victim Faith Parker has no memory of her near-fatal crash, of her previous life, or of her friend, journalist Dinah Leighton, who mysteriously vanished after visiting her in the hospital, but as she recovers, she begins to experience intimate dreams of a man she does not recognize and horrifying visions of deadly violence. Original.

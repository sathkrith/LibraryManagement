title:Witchfire
category:Fiction
abstract:In this sequel to best-selling 'Deceptions,' Connor Hawthorne, attorney turned crime novelist, departs from her native Santa Fe to travel to England, where grave robbers have looted the grave of her recently deceased grandmother. Connor is not prepared for the family secrets that come tumbling out and threaten not only her, but everyone she loves. Blending thriller fiction with mythology, Lauren Maddison adds in some murder and builds the story to a heart-pounding conclusion.

title:A Temporary New Wife
category:Fiction
abstract:Sixty-four year-old Wilson believes he has discovered an open, honest way to develop short-term relationships with younger women. Translating his idea into reality, he finds that events do not always turn out as he expected; however, others take an interest in his plan and try to follow suit. The result is a dark but often hilarious comedy that carries Wilson and friends through their highly unconventional amorous adventures. Original.

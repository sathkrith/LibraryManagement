title:Lady in Green and Minor Indiscretions
category:Fiction
abstract:Escaping an arranged marriage, Annalise Avery takes employment at Lord Gardiner's townhouse and, disguised as his housekeeper, vows to end the lord's lascivious ways in Lady in Green, while in Minor Indiscretions, Melody Ashton is stunned to be accused of blackmail by the rakish Lord Coe, who then offers to make her his mistress. Original.

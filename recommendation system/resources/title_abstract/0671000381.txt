title:Courtney Love
category:Biography & Autobiography
abstract:The astonishing no-holds-barred look into the heart and soul of today's high priestess of rock n' roll, this book reveals the true Courtney Love, lead singer of the rock group, Hole, and late wife of Kurt Cobain, the lead singer of Nirvana. Love was institutionalized as a juvenile delinquent, tormented as a rock groupie and nearly driven to suicide as a grieving widow.
